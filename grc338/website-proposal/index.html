Introduction

Lavender Jewelry

Lavender Jewelry is an ethically sourced, reasonably priced, gold jewelry store handmade with love. In hopes to promote sustainability while giving individuals a piece of beauty.

Target audience

Primary target would be young adult women who are looking to find ethical and sustainable jewelry, but are on a budget. Women between the age of 20-40 would be the ideal target.

The target is provide jewelry that is not too expensive or high end, but is still able to be ethically sourced and morally created. The goal is motivate individuals to support small businesses and companies that are conscious in how they supply their products while giving a cheaper alternative.

Our story

Lavender Jewelry started in 2014 with the intentions of creating pieces that are ethically sourced, but as reasonably priced it could be. Our store has become a local favorite and has since been able to find more and more ethical sources for the jewelry. As we grow bigger, we love to partner with local businesses and donate to organizations that align with Lavender Jewelry's values.
